What is Subscription?

Premium pet subscription (e.g. Pixel Cat Subscription) gives you access to your adorable pet companions that appear on your screen and bring joy to your daily device usage.

How It Works

After Subscription

  1. Activate Subscription: Subscribe through Google Play Store
  2. Grant Permissions: Allow screen overlay permission
  3. Customize Your Pet: Set size and activity in the carousel
  4. Let It Wander: Tap "Let your pet wander" to activate
  5. Enjoy: Your pet appears on screen across all apps

Subscription Management

  • Easy Activation: Subscribe directly in the app
  • Multiple Subscriptions: Manage different pet subscriptions individually
  • Direct Play Store Access: Click on any pet subscription to go directly to Play Store management
  • Google Play Billing: Secure payment through Google Play Store
  • Auto-Renewal: Each subscription continues until you cancel
  • Easy Cancellation: Cancel individual subscriptions anytime in Google Play Store settings

Pricing and Billing

Transparent Pricing

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I try before I subscribe?
A: Yes! You can use the free pets to experience the app before subscribing to premium pets.
Q: Will my pet disappear if I cancel?
A: Your pet will remain active until your current subscription period ends.
Q: Can I use multiple pets at once?
A: Yes! You can have multiple pets active at the same time on your screen for an even more delightful experience.
Q: Does the subscription include future pets?
A: Each pet has its own separate subscription. Future pets will have their own individual subscriptions that you can manage independently.
