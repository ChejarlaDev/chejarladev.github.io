Privacy Policy for Discount Calculator

Last updated: August 6th, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Discount Calculator ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

Personal Information

Our app does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Addresses
  • Payment information
  • Any other personally identifiable information

User Input Data

The app collects temporary input data that you enter while using the calculator:

  • Original price amounts
  • Discount percentages
  • Tax percentages

Important:

This input data is:

  • Processed locally on your device only
  • Not stored permanently
  • Not transmitted to any external servers
  • Automatically cleared when you close the app

Device Information

We may collect basic device information through Google AdMob for advertising purposes:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Advertising identifier (if available)

How We Use Information

Calculator Functionality

Your input data is used solely for:

  • Performing discount calculations
  • Displaying results in real-time
  • No other purpose

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device information for ad targeting
  • Ad interaction data
  • Performance metrics

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

Our app integrates Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob's privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can review Google's Privacy Policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Storage and Security

Local Processing

All calculations are performed locally on your device. No user data is:

  • Stored on our servers
  • Transmitted over the internet
  • Shared with third parties (except as noted for advertising)

Data Retention

Since we don't store your data, there is no data retention period. Your input is automatically cleared when you close the app.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal information, there is no personal data to:

  • Access
  • Correct
  • Delete
  • Port
  • Restrict processing

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed to be safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: chejarla.apps@gmail.com
  • Developer: Jyothi Chejarla

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, our legal basis for processing your data is:

  • Legitimate Interest: Providing calculator functionality
  • Consent: For advertising purposes (handled by Google AdMob)

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

Since we don't collect personal information, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) does not apply to our data practices.

International Data Transfers

We do not transfer any user data internationally as all processing occurs locally on your device.

Data Breach Procedures

Since we don't store user data, there is no risk of data breaches related to user information. However, if we become aware of any security issues, we will promptly notify users through app updates.

By using our Discount Calculator app, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
