Privacy Policy for Pixel Petverse

Effective Date: October 8, 2025
Last Updated: October 8, 2025

Introduction

We believe your privacy is paramount. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Pixel Petverse ("the App").

Information We Collect

Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Personal Data: We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Usage Tracking: We do not track your app usage patterns or behavior
  • Device Information: We do not collect device-specific identifiers
  • Location Data: We do not access or store your location information
  • Third-Party Data: We do not share any information with third parties for marketing purposes

Information We DO Collect

  • Local Preferences: Pet selection, size settings, and behavior preferences are stored locally on your device only
  • Subscription Status: Through RevenueCat and Google Play Store for subscription management

Third-Party Services

RevenueCat & Google Play Store

  • Purpose: Secure subscription and payment processing
  • Data Handled: Subscription status and payment transactions
  • Security: All payment information is processed securely by Google Play Store
  • Storage: No payment information is stored on our servers
  • Privacy: RevenueCat and Google Play Store have their own privacy policies

No Other Third-Party Services

  • No Analytics: We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or similar services
  • No Advertising: We do not use advertising networks or collect data for ads
  • No Social Media: We do not integrate with social media platforms

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only

  • All your preferences (pet selection, size, activities) are stored locally on your device
  • No personal data is transmitted to our servers
  • Your pet customization settings remain private to you

Subscription Data

  • Subscription status is managed securely through RevenueCat and Google Play Store
  • We only receive anonymized subscription status information
  • No payment details are accessible to us

Children's Privacy

Pixel Petverse is designed to be family-friendly and safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

Your Rights

Data Control

  • Local Control: You have complete control over your local app preferences
  • Subscription Management: Manage your subscription through Google Play Store
  • Data Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all local data

No Data Requests Needed

Since we don't collect personal data, there's no need to request data deletion or access from us.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Website: https://chejarla.in

Our Commitment: Your privacy and happiness are our top priorities. We build apps to bring joy, not to collect data.
